First Article

Posted on 3 February 2023. This is about Duffy the Disney Bear's creation story!

Duffy's current creation story started a long, long time ago in 2004. Mickey Mouse was lonely because he was a sea captain and missed his friends while sailing. So one day, Minnie Mouse decided to sew a special teddy bear for him to bring on sailing trips in a duffel bag. That's why Mickey decided to name him Duffy! Duffy has a hip Mickey birthmark and the shape of Mickey's face. One night when Mickey was asleep, he dreamt that Duffy magically came alive in a sailor costume, and said that they would go on adventures together. The next morning, he was shocked when he woke up to find Duffy looking exactly like he was in the dream! Duffy is Mickey's special buddy and accompanies him on many adventures, so now Mickey is not lonely anymore. Mickey swears that even to this day, when he turns around, he catches Duffy peeking and winking at him! Feel free to find out more here at wikipedia.

Second Article

Posted on 3 February 2023.

Duffy the Disney Bear is popular and can be found in:

  • Tokyo Disney Resort
  • Hong Kong Disneyland
  • Disneyland Paris
  • ShangHai Disneyland
  • Disney Cruise Line

You can also find Duffy the Disney Bear and his friends at park attractions such as "Meet and Greet",shows,parades and celebrations! But be prepared as there will definitely be long queues and huge crowds!
